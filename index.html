Telecom Customer Churn Prediction

Business Problem: Telecom companies face revenue loss due to customer churn. The goal was to predict customers likely to cancel services and enable proactive retention strategies.

Approach:

  • Analyzed 7,000+ customer records with 21 features
  • Performed data cleaning and feature engineering
  • Built Logistic Regression and XGBoost models
  • Evaluated performance using accuracy, precision, and recall

Key Results:

  • Achieved ~80% prediction accuracy
  • XGBoost improved churn detection performance
  • Delivered actionable insights for targeted retention campaigns

Tech Stack: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, Seaborn, Matplotlib
